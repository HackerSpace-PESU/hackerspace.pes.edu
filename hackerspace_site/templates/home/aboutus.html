What is HackerSpace?


HackerSpace is a community-powered Development Club based in PES University, RR Campus which was formed in 2019 by the batch of â€˜22. At HackerSpace, we are more than a club. We are a Community. The Club aims to inspire peers and create a strong development-oriented community within the campus by promoting community powered development.

Since we believe in collective growth and participation, all activities are organized by the peers, for the peers. We provide a platform for peers to interact with one another and work together to develop applications that can change lives.

Club Objectives:

  • To create a strong development-oriented community on campus
  • To build a unifying platform for collective growth
  • To introduce peers to various domains and their applications
  • To train and build the skills required to develop domain experience
  • To develop an affinity towards open-source contributions
