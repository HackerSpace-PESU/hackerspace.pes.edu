About Us


HackerSpace presents its first-ever hackathon - SpaceJam 2021

SpaceJam is open to all students across India. Since we're starting off, the theme is Open Innovation - build out the great ideas you have to solve the problems you face every day! No restrictions at all! This is a beginner-friendly hackathon, and aims to provide a great platform for first-timers to explore the world of hackathons while allowing experienced pros to showcase their talents.

This hackathon will be aimed at freshers, providing the new batch of students with their first experience in ideating, brainstorming and developing for a hackathon. This will also introduce them to new technologies and provide a challenging experience to build their ideas as well. They will be mentored by the HackerSpace team throughout the duration of the event, so they can learn more about how to proceed and go about a Hackathon and still walk away a winner.

The HackerSpace organisation functions out of PES University, Bangalore, but since this event will be conducted virtually, participants of any age or institution are welcome to contest in SpaceJam!
