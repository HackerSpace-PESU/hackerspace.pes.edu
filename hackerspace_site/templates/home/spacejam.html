About Us


HackerSpace presents its first-ever hackathon - SpaceJam 2021

The hackathon is open to all students of PES University. The theme is Open Innovation, so build out on the great ideas that you have to solve problems that you face every day!

This Hackathon will be aimed at being beginner-friendly that aims provide the new batch of students with their first experience in ideating and developing and to explore the world of hackathon.

We also aim to introduce them to new technologies in addition to providing them with a challenge while also allowing experienced pros to showcase their talents.
